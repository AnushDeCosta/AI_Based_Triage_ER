Project Overview

The project aims to build a machine learning model that automates and enhances the triage process in emergency departments by accurately assessing patients’ urgency levels. The model will be trained on historical patient data, including demographics, presenting symptoms, vital signs, and clinical outcomes. Once developed, the system will be integrated into the hospital’s electronic health record system and evaluated for effectiveness.
